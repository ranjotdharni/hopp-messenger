Get Started

Welcome to ChemiStreams! We offer a free, open-source, browser-to-browser messaging service with a musically personalised dashboard. Want to get started right away? Click below to open your own, personal ChemiStreams dashboard. You will have to make an account first.

Want to see the source code for this website? Check it out on GitHub! Click below to see the repository. Want more details on the service? Discover new music that you'll love based on what you're currently listening to; scroll further and read below on the features it offers, or try it for yourself!

Send one-on-one messages to your friends via their ChemiStreams account in the browser. Create chatrooms and invite more friends for larger conversations. Connect with friends and share your musical interests using fast, real-time WebSocket communication.

Set 'Beacon' artists, tracks, and genres to generate personalised music recommendations. Our algorithm will then communicate with the Spotify API, and fill your dashboard accordingly with the returned resources. You will need a Spotify Premium account to fully use this feature.

Connect to your Spotify account to have your Beacons set automatically for an even more personalised experience. ChemiStreams will NEVER store your private credentials for any third-party services.
